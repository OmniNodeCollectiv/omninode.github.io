FAQ

What if I only have 10 animals to track?

Use Observe (free). Upgrade to Shield when you have 50+ animals or need alerts.

Can I switch tiers?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade monthly. Sovereign is annual.

Who owns my data?

You own your raw sensor data. Aggregated data is co-owned per the Ethical License.

What if OmniNode shuts down?

The protocol is MIT-licensed. All KaaS code is open-source within 1 year. Your deployment continues.

Can I get a discount?

Conservation Offset applies: deploy nodes instead of paying full price.
